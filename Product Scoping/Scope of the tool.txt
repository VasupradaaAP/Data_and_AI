FUNCTIONALITIES OF THE INTERNAL TOOL
1. What the tool actually is
This is an internal marketing intelligence layer that sits on top of the tools the team already uses. The team keeps using Excel, Power BI, Slack, and every other tool in their stack. This tool does not touch their workflow. It plugs into the data those tools already produce.

2. Who it is for
For the Primary User: The Marketing Manager
The tool automatically connects to all ad platforms, balances out the different metrics, and flags exactly where you are losing or winning money. Instead of spending hours hunting for problems, the manager simply reviews the automated findings, approves the best strategy, and prepares for the team meeting in minutes.
For the Secondary User: The Team
The team doesn't need to log into complex software or decipher spreadsheets. The tool packages the final, manager-approved insights into a simple Slack message or a clean PDF. They instantly see what is working, what isn't, and what the plan is.

Step 5: Multi-Channel KPI Comparison
Teams evaluate core channel metrics—including ROAS, CTR, conversions, and CAC—to establish a baseline of current performance across marketing platforms.
AI Agent Integration + MCP: The AI agent uses MCP-based integrations to automatically collect and normalize cross-channel marketing data, benchmark key performance metrics, and generate comparative insights that clearly identify which channels are outperforming and which are falling behind.

Step 7: Advanced Performance Analysis
Marketing analysts and managers dive deeper into the data to track trends, isolate top-performing assets, and diagnose underperforming campaigns.
AI Agent Integration: Predictive Analysis & Strategic Forecasting. The AI agent automatically calculates profit/loss margins, identifies macro trends, and delivers actionable recommendations on how to optimize current performance and where to focus future resources.

Step 9: Agile Decision-Making & Optimization
Final strategic decisions are executed regarding budget reallocation, scaling high-performing channels, or pausing underperforming efforts.
Hybrid Integration (AI Agent + Manual Discussion): Human-in-the-Loop Execution. The AI agent generates data-driven budget scenarios and optimization roadmaps, which the team and clients review, refine, and manually approve for final deployment.

Step 10: Reporting & Sharing
Marketing teams compile final reports, interactive dashboards, or presentation decks to communicate outcomes with managers, leadership, or clients.
AI Agent Integration: Automated Document Creation & Distribution. The AI agent automatically compiles performance data into polished reports, builds dynamic executive dashboards, and handles the scheduled distribution of these assets to key clients and internal stakeholders.
